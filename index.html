About Me

I’m a Certified Usability Analyst (CUA®) by Human Factors International (HFI), with 7+ years of experience across UI/UX, Graphic Design, and Interior Design - a journey that has shaped how I understand people, spaces, and digital experiences.

Based in Pune, I design intuitive, accessible, and scalable solutions, focusing on simplifying complex systems through user research, structured thinking, and usability-driven decisions.

Over the years, I’ve led UX initiatives for large-scale platforms, collaborating with cross-functional teams to turn real-world problems into meaningful digital experiences. From improving information architecture to ensuring accessibility compliance, I approach design with both empathy and clarity.

I actively use AI to work more efficiently and enjoy vibe coding to build and experiment with ideas - just like this website.

What drives me most is understanding how users think, behave, and interact and transforming those insights into experiences that feel effortless.

Beyond design, I enjoy exploring creativity in different forms whether it’s experimenting in the kitchen, illustrating ideas, or getting lost in a good horror movie. (The Blair Witch Project and The Exorcism of Emily Rose are my favorites).
